Who I Am

Science trained.
Visually driven.

My name is Onyebuchi Genius Okonkwo. I studied Zoology at Nnamdi Azikiwe University, with a focus on parasitology and public health. My professional path since then has been shaped by a simple but consistent observation: information only makes a difference when it reaches people clearly.

During my year as a National Health Fellow with the Federal Ministry of Health in Nkanu West LGA, I spent a significant part of my time translating field data and assessment results into reports, presentations, and visual materials that state health authorities and community members could actually act on. I was not hired as a designer — but the work demanded design thinking.

Through SDG advocacy programmes and a Graphic Design facilitation role, I developed a hands-on understanding of layout principles, colour use, and print production. My work is still growing, and I am here precisely because I want to develop it properly — inside a team that takes design seriously. I come with an open mind, genuine curiosity, and no illusions about being finished.
